I have been fascinated with tech for the longest of times. It started off with playing games, to building computers, to creating websites with the 'what you see is what you get' website builders. I have always thought that my future was in tech, but alas, pressures of the family pushed me alway from that future. Hence, being a teacher. I love all my kids, however, if I am telling them to chase their dreams to be happy, then I have to follow that advice too.

As everyone says, it's never too late to do what you love. And after 10 years of moving up the teaching chain, here I am. Back to being a student and having a blast learning. I am soaking all the information I can, while trying not to bang my head against the wall too much.
